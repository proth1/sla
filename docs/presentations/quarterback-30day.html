Why Onboarding Takes 6+ Months
Three root causes drive 80% of the delay
1

18 Sequential Committees

Every request passes through up to 18 review committees one at a time. Security waits for Legal. Legal waits for Compliance. A 5-day review becomes 90 days of elapsed time.

2

No Single Point of Accountability

Nobody owns elapsed time. Each reviewer owns their piece, but nobody tracks the overall request. When it stalls, no one notices for weeks.

3

Everything Gets Full Treatment

A $10K SaaS renewal with no regulated data gets the same 9-reviewer due diligence as a $2M AI platform handling regulated data from a new vendor. It's not just dollars—a low-risk renewal and a high-risk AI deployment carry fundamentally different risk profiles, yet both sit in the same queue. 40-50% could be fast-tracked.

Week 1-2: Stop the Bleeding
Three changes that can start on Day 1
#1

Appoint Onboarding Quarterbacks (Day 1)

Assign 2-3 dedicated facilitators who own each request end-to-end. Track where every active request sits, chase non-responsive reviewers, escalate stalls after 48 hours, report weekly to leadership.

IMPACT: Visibility alone cuts delays
#2

Kill Sequential Reviews — Run Parallel (Day 3)

Send the review package to each reviewer as soon as their prerequisite data is ready. Reviewers work in parallel whenever possible—but no one is asked to start until their required intake information is complete and available.

IMPACT: Compresses Due Diligence from 8-12 weeks to 2-3 weeks. This single change is worth 2-3 months.
#3

Paper Pre-Screen (Mini RFP)

1-page intake form with 5 deal-killers before pipeline entry: Budget approval? Executive sponsor (VP+)? Regulated data? Existing contract? Realistic go-live? If first two are "No", request does not enter.

IMPACT: Filters 20-30% at intake

#2 Parallel Reviews

Single biggest lever. Engage reviewers as soon as their prerequisite data is ready—work in parallel, not sequentially. No budget, no technology, no reorg — just a process decision.

Creates ownership where none exists. A single person tracking elapsed time and chasing blockers changes behavior instantly.

Toolkit #1
Intake Pre-Screen Form

Deal-Killer Checklist

Budget approval obtained (Minimum VP-level sign-off)
Executive sponsor identified (VP+ level)
Data classification determined (sensitive data identified)
Existing contract or vendor relationship documented
Realistic go-live date (>30 days from intake)

If either of the first two are unchecked, request does not enter the pipeline.

Data Classification

Contains sensitive or regulated data
Contains financial or payment data
Subject to GDPR / CCPA
Contains proprietary / trade secret data

All 9 reviewers + executive briefing. 25-day SLA. Critical infrastructure, AI platforms, regulated data.
